what are the subsets of SQL?
There are 4 subsets of SQL:
Data definition language:
it consist of commands that are related to defining the schema of the database.

Data manipulation language:
it consist of the commands that are used to manipulate the data present in the database.

Data control language:
it handles all the commands related to rights , permissions and other controls of the database sys.

Transaction control language:
it includes commands that are mainly related to the trasactions in databse.
